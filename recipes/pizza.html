Pizza Recipe

The best homemade pizza recipe! Get the perfect hot and cripsy pizza from scratch to make the perfect pepperoni pizza!

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Pizza sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Pepperoni
  • Parmasan cheese

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 550 degrees
  2. Prepare work surface
  3. Roll out dough
  4. Transfer pizza to pan
  5. Add toppings
  6. Put into oven and bake for 8-12 minutes
